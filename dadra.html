Dadra Taal

6 Beats (Matras) | 2 Sections (Vibhags) | Popular in Light Classical & Ghazals

Matra (Beat) Devanagari (हिन्दी/मराठी) English Transliteration Sign (Tali/Khali) Listen
1 धा Dhā Sam / Taali (1)
2 धिं Dhiṅ -
3 ना Nā -
4 ता Tā Khaali (0)
5 तिं Tiṅ -
6 ना Nā -

Structure (विभाग)

Dadra Taal mein kul 6 matrayen hoti hain, jo 3-3 matraon ke do vibhagon mein baanti gayi hain. Pehli matra par Taali hoti hai (Sam) aur chauthi (4th) matra par Khaali hoti hai.

Application (उपयोग)

Yeh taal zyada tar Semi-Classical music, Ghazals, Bhajans, aur Bollywood ke purane gaano mein sunne ko milti hai. Iska chalan kaafi tez aur chanchal hota hai.
