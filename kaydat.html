What is a Kaayda?

Kayda means "rule" or "discipline." It is a composition that originated in the Delhi Gharana. A Kayda is built around a main theme (mukhda), and all its variations (paltas) are created by rearranging and developing only the bols (syllables) of that original theme. The fundamental rule is that no new or external bols may be added; every variation must use only the bols from the original composition
